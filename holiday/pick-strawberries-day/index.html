Overview

Pick Strawberries Day, observed annually on May 20th, is an unofficial holiday that celebrates the joy of harvesting fresh strawberries. It encourages individuals and families to visit local farms and pick their own berries, embracing the peak of strawberry season. This day promotes appreciation for local agriculture, healthy eating, and the simple pleasure of gathering these sweet, vibrant fruits directly from the field.

Observed each year on May 20, Pick Strawberries Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Strawberries are the only fruit with their seeds on the outside.

Botanically, a strawberry is not a true berry but an 'aggregate accessory fruit' because its seeds are on the exterior.
