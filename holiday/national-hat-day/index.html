Overview

National Hat Day, observed annually on January 15th, is an unofficial holiday dedicated to celebrating headwear in all its forms. It encourages people to don their favorite hats, appreciate the history and diversity of head coverings, and perhaps even learn about different hat styles and their cultural significance. From practical beanies to stylish fedoras, this day is a lighthearted occasion to express personality or simply enjoy the warmth and fashion statement a hat can provide.

Observed each year on January 15, National Hat Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
