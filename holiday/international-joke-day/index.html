Overview

International Joke Day, observed annually on July 1st, is an informal global celebration dedicated to sharing humor and laughter. It encourages people to tell jokes, riddles, and funny stories to friends, family, and colleagues, fostering a spirit of lightheartedness and joy. Unlike some official holidays, its origins are somewhat obscure, but it serves as a popular reminder of the universal appeal and benefits of humor.

Observed each year on July 1, International Joke Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

While widely observed on July 1st, the precise origin of International Joke Day remains largely unverified, making it more of a cultural tradition than an officially declared holiday.
