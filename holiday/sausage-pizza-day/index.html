Overview

Sausage Pizza Day is an unofficial, culinary observance celebrated annually on October 11th. It's a day dedicated to enjoying and appreciating the versatile and delicious combination of sausage and pizza, encouraging enthusiasts to savor this beloved topping in its many forms, from spicy Italian sausage to savory crumbles, on various crusts and with different sauce combinations.

Observed each year on October 11, Sausage Pizza Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Sausage consistently ranks among the top five most popular pizza toppings worldwide.

Italian sausage, often seasoned with fennel or anise, is a traditional and widely favored variety for pizza.
