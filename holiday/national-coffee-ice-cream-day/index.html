Overview

National Coffee Ice Cream Day, observed annually on September 6th, is a delicious holiday dedicated to celebrating the rich and creamy frozen dessert that blends the bold flavor of coffee with the sweet indulgence of ice cream. It's a perfect occasion for enthusiasts to enjoy this unique treat, whether in a cone, a cup, or as part of a more elaborate dessert.

Observed each year on September 6, National Coffee Ice Cream Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Coffee-flavored ice cream is thought to have originated in Europe by the mid-18th century, with recipes appearing in French cookbooks.

It consistently ranks among the top 10 to 15 most popular ice cream flavors globally, cherished for its distinct bittersweet profile.
