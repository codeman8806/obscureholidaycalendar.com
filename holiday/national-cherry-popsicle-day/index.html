Overview

National Cherry Popsicle Day, observed annually on August 26th, celebrates the beloved frozen treat in its vibrant red, fruity, and refreshing cherry flavor. This unofficial holiday encourages people to cool down and enjoy the simple pleasure of a cherry-flavored popsicle, perfect for a late summer indulgence.

Observed each year on August 26, National Cherry Popsicle Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

The first frozen treat on a stick was accidentally invented in 1905 by 11-year-old Frank Epperson.

Epperson originally called his invention an 'Epsicle,' later changing it to 'Popsicle' at his children's suggestion.
