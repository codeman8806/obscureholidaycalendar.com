Overview

Coconut Cream Pie Day, observed annually on May 8th, is a delightful occasion dedicated to celebrating the rich, tropical dessert. This classic pie features a flaky pastry crust filled with a smooth, sweet custard infused with coconut, often topped with a generous layer of whipped cream and toasted coconut flakes, offering a perfect blend of creamy texture and exotic flavor that is enjoyed by many.

Observed each year on May 8, Coconut Cream Pie Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Coconuts are botanically classified as a fibrous drupe, not a true nut.

The 'cream pie' designation typically refers to pies filled with a thickened custard or pudding, often without baking the filling once assembled in the crust.
