Overview

National Eat Your Beans Day, celebrated annually on July 3rd, is an unofficial holiday dedicated to encouraging people to enjoy the diverse and nutritious world of beans. It highlights their versatility as a culinary ingredient and their numerous health benefits, such as being a great source of protein, fiber, and essential minerals, while also being a sustainable and affordable food choice.

Observed each year on July 3, National Eat Your Beans Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Beans are a nutritional powerhouse, providing significant amounts of plant-based protein, fiber, and essential vitamins and minerals.

There are thousands of bean varieties worldwide, ranging from common kidney and black beans to more exotic types, each with unique flavors and textures.
