Overview

National Buffet Day, celebrated on January 2nd, is a delectable occasion dedicated to the art and joy of all-you-can-eat dining. It's a day to indulge in a wide variety of dishes, from savory appetizers to sweet desserts, allowing everyone to customize their meal exactly to their liking without limits.

Observed each year on January 2, National Buffet Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
