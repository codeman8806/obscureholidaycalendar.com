Overview

"Stupid Guy Thing Day" is a fictional, humorous "holiday" invented by the character Michael Scott in the American television series *The Office*. As described by Michael, it supposedly falls on June 22nd and is a day when men can engage in foolish or nonsensical activities and "get away with it." It is not an officially recognized public or cultural holiday but serves as a comedic element within the show.

Observed each year on June 22, Stupid Guy Thing Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

The "holiday" was first introduced in "The Job, Part 2," the third-season finale of *The Office* (US).

Michael Scott created the concept to explain an activity he had scheduled on June 22nd.
