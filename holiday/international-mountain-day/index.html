International Mountain Day

December 11

What is International Mountain Day?

International Mountain Day, observed annually on December 11th, is a day dedicated to raising awareness about the importance of mountains to life, highlighting the opportunities and constraints in mountain development, and building partnerships that will bring positive change to the world's mountains and highlands.

History of International Mountain Day

International Mountain Day doesn't have a long formal history like major public holidays, but it has grown in popularity thanks to social media, blogs, and people who love celebrating the little things in life. As more creators, families, and teachers look for reasons to mark each day with something fun, International Mountain Day has become one of those niche observances that quietly spreads through word of mouth and online posts.

Like many modern “national days,” International Mountain Day is part of a larger movement of unofficial holidays that help people connect, share laughs, and create small traditions around specific dates on the calendar.
