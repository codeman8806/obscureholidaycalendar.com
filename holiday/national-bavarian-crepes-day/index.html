Overview

National Bavarian Crepes Day, observed annually on March 22nd, invites enthusiasts to celebrate and enjoy crepes, particularly those filled with Bavarian-inspired ingredients or, most famously, rich Bavarian cream. This unofficial food holiday blends the delicate French crepe with the creamy, custard-like dessert filling, offering a delightful culinary experience.

Observed each year on March 22, National Bavarian Crepes Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Bavarian cream is a dessert made from milk, sugar, eggs, and gelatin, often flavored with vanilla and lightened with whipped cream.

Crepes originated in Brittany, France, and are traditionally much thinner than their American pancake counterparts.
