International Coffee Day ☕️

October 1 Cultural / community observance Updated 2025-12-08

This holiday is featured in the Obscure Holiday Calendar app with emoji-style visuals, reminders, and daily fun facts.

Overview

International Coffee Day, observed annually on October 1st, is a global celebration dedicated to acknowledging and appreciating coffee as one of the world's most beloved beverages. It serves as an occasion to promote fair trade practices, raise awareness about the challenges faced by coffee growers, and honor the millions of people worldwide involved in the coffee industry, from farmers to baristas.

Observed each year on October 1, International Coffee Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

International Coffee Day was officially launched by the International Coffee Organization (ICO) on October 1, 2015.

Globally, an estimated 2.25 billion cups of coffee are consumed daily.
