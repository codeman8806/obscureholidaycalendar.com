Overview

National Raspberry Cake Day is celebrated annually on July 31st, inviting people to indulge in the delightful combination of sweet cake and the tart, fruity flavor of raspberries. This culinary holiday encourages baking and enjoying raspberry-infused cakes, whether they feature fresh berries, raspberry jam, or a rich raspberry filling, highlighting one of summer's beloved fruits in a classic dessert.

Observed each year on July 31, National Raspberry Cake Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Raspberries are aggregate fruits, meaning they are made up of many tiny drupelets, each with its own seed.

While red raspberries are most common, varieties also exist in black, purple, and golden colors.
