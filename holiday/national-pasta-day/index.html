Overview

National Pasta Day, observed annually on October 17th, is a culinary celebration dedicated to one of the world's most beloved and versatile food staples. It encourages individuals to enjoy their favorite pasta dishes, explore new recipes, and appreciate the cultural significance and diverse forms of pasta, from classic spaghetti to intricate stuffed varieties. The day highlights pasta's role in global cuisine, its comforting appeal, and its ability to bring people together over a shared meal.

Observed each year on October 17, National Pasta Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

There are over 600 distinct shapes of pasta produced worldwide.
