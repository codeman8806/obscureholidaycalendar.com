Overview

Be Bald and Be Free Day, observed annually on October 14th, is a lighthearted occasion dedicated to celebrating baldness and the liberation it can bring. It encourages individuals who are bald, or those who appreciate the look, to embrace their appearance with confidence and to enjoy the freedom from societal pressures, hair-related routines, and the often considerable expense of hair care. The day champions self-acceptance and challenges conventional beauty standards, highlighting the unique style and ease that comes with being bald.

Observed each year on October 14, Be Bald and Be Free Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Many prominent historical and contemporary figures, from Julius Caesar to Jeff Bezos, have been famously bald.

Going bald can save individuals a significant amount of time and money that would otherwise be spent on hair products, styling, and haircuts.
