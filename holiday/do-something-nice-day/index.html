Do Something Nice Day ❤️

October 5 Cultural / community observance Updated 2025-12-16

This holiday is featured in the Obscure Holiday Calendar app with emoji-style visuals, reminders, and daily fun facts.

Overview

Do Something Nice Day, widely observed annually on May 10th, encourages individuals to perform acts of kindness, whether big or small, to spread positivity and make a meaningful difference in the lives of others. It serves as a heartwarming reminder that simple gestures of goodwill can create a significant ripple effect throughout communities, fostering a more compassionate and connected world.

Observed each year on October 5, Do Something Nice Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

While the precise origins of this particular observance are not widely documented, its spirit aligns with global movements promoting kindness and altruism.

Engaging in acts of kindness is scientifically linked to improved well-being for both the giver and the receiver, boosting mood and reducing stress.
