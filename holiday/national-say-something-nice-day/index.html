Overview

National Say Something Nice Day, observed annually on June 1st, encourages individuals to spread positivity and kindness by intentionally complimenting others or offering uplifting words. This observance aims to highlight the powerful impact of positive communication on personal well-being and community spirit, fostering an environment of appreciation and goodwill.

Observed each year on June 1, National Say Something Nice Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

National Say Something Nice Day was founded by former Alabama Governor Bob Riley.

Studies suggest that giving compliments can boost the mood of both the person giving and the person receiving them.
