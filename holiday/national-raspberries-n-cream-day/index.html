Overview

National Raspberries N' Cream Day, observed annually on August 7th, is a delightful occasion dedicated to celebrating the refreshing and classic pairing of fresh, ripe raspberries with rich, smooth cream. This simple yet elegant dessert is enjoyed in various forms, from fresh berries topped with whipped cream to more elaborate parfaits and tarts, honoring its enduring popularity as a summer treat.

Observed each year on August 7, National Raspberries N' Cream Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
