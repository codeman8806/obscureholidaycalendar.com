Overview

National Fast Food Day, observed annually on November 16th, is an unofficial holiday dedicated to celebrating the convenience, widespread availability, and cultural phenomenon of fast food. It encourages people to indulge in their favorite quick-service meals, from burgers and fries to tacos and pizza, acknowledging the industry's significant impact on dining habits worldwide.

Observed each year on November 16, National Fast Food Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story
