Overview

International Kissing Day, observed annually on July 6th, celebrates the simple yet profound act of kissing as a universal expression of love, affection, friendship, and passion. It encourages people worldwide to appreciate the emotional and physical connection that a kiss can convey, fostering intimacy and strengthening bonds between individuals.

Observed each year on July 6, International Kissing Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
