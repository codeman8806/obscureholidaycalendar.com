Overview

Embrace Your Geekness Day, observed annually on July 13th, is a celebration of individuality, niche interests, and the unique passions that define a 'geek.' It encourages people to proudly showcase their enthusiasm for topics ranging from science fiction and fantasy to technology, gaming, comics, and specialized academic subjects, recognizing that deep knowledge and earnest enjoyment are qualities to be celebrated rather than hidden.

Observed each year on July 13, Embrace Your Geekness Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

The word 'geek' originally referred to circus performers known for biting the heads off live chickens, evolving over time to describe an intellectual enthusiast.

Modern 'geek culture,' encompassing interests like sci-fi, gaming, and comics, now drives a multi-billion dollar entertainment industry.
