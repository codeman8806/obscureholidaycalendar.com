Overview

Bulldogs Are Beautiful Day, observed annually on April 21st, is a special occasion dedicated to celebrating the distinctive charm and unique aesthetic of bulldog breeds. This day encourages appreciation for their iconic wrinkled faces, sturdy builds, and often endearing temperaments, highlighting the deep affection and loyalty these dogs offer. It serves as an opportunity for enthusiasts and owners to recognize the beauty within these beloved companions and advocate for their welfare.

Observed each year on April 21, Bulldogs Are Beautiful Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Modern Bulldogs are celebrated for their gentle and loyal disposition, a stark contrast to their ancestors' original purpose in bull-baiting.

The English Bulldog is often regarded as a national symbol of Great Britain, representing tenacity and courage.
