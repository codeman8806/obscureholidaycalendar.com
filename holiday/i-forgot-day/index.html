Overview

I Forgot Day, observed annually on July 2nd, is a lighthearted, unofficial holiday dedicated to the universal experience of forgetting things. It serves as a humorous reminder to acknowledge and perhaps even forgive oneself for those minor lapses in memory, whether it's a forgotten appointment, a misplaced item, or a missed deadline. The day encourages a chuckle rather than frustration over the inevitable human tendency to forget.

Observed each year on July 2, I Forgot Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
