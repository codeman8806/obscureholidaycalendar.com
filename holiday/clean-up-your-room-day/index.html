Clean Up Your Room Day ✨

May 10 Cultural / community observance Updated 2025-12-08

This holiday is featured in the Obscure Holiday Calendar app with emoji-style visuals, reminders, and daily fun facts.

Overview

Clean Up Your Room Day, observed annually on May 10th, is an unofficial holiday encouraging individuals, particularly children, to organize and tidy their personal living spaces. It serves as a light-hearted reminder to declutter, establish good habits for maintaining order, and appreciate the benefits of a clean environment, which can contribute to a more focused and peaceful state of mind.

Observed each year on May 10, Clean Up Your Room Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

While its exact origin is largely unknown, "Clean Up Your Room Day" has been a popular unofficial observance since at least the late 20th century.

Studies have indicated that living in a cluttered environment can increase stress and anxiety, whereas an organized space can improve concentration and reduce mental fatigue.
