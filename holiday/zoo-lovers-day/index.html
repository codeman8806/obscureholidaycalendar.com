Overview

Zoo Lovers Day, observed annually on April 8th, is a dedicated occasion to celebrate and appreciate zoos, aquariums, and wildlife parks worldwide. It encourages individuals to visit these institutions, learn about the diverse animal species they house, understand the critical roles zoos play in conservation, research, and education, and support their ongoing efforts to protect endangered species and preserve biodiversity.

Observed each year on April 8, Zoo Lovers Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
