Overview

Fresh Veggies Day, observed annually on June 16th, is a celebration dedicated to encouraging people worldwide to incorporate more fresh vegetables into their diets. It highlights the numerous health benefits, vibrant flavors, and versatility of vegetables, urging individuals to choose nutrient-rich options for improved well-being and to appreciate the bounty of nature's produce.

Observed each year on June 16, Fresh Veggies Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
