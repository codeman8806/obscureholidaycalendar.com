Overview

Be a Millionaire Day, observed annually on May 20th, encourages individuals to dream big about financial success and to take proactive steps towards achieving their monetary goals. It serves as an inspirational reminder to explore financial planning, entrepreneurship, and smart investment strategies, prompting people to visualize and work towards a future of wealth and security.

Observed each year on May 20, Be a Millionaire Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Many self-made millionaires attribute their wealth to consistent saving, investing early, and living below their means.

The term 'millionaire' first appeared in the English language in 1816.
