Absolutely Incredible Kid Day ⭐

March 21 Cultural / community observance Updated 2025-12-07

This holiday is featured in the Obscure Holiday Calendar app with artwork, reminders, and daily fun facts.

Overview

"Absolutely Incredible Kid Day" is an annual holiday observed on March 21st, encouraging adults to write letters of encouragement, praise, and support to the young people in their lives. Founded by the Camp Fire organization, the day aims to boost children's self-esteem and remind them of their value and potential, fostering positive relationships and development through simple, heartfelt communication.

Observed each year on March 21, Absolutely Incredible Kid Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

The holiday was first celebrated in 1993.

It was established by the Camp Fire organization, a youth development nonprofit.
