Overview

National Underwear Day, observed annually on August 5th, is an unofficial holiday dedicated to celebrating the essential undergarment worn by most people. While not a public holiday, it's often recognized on social media as a lighthearted occasion to appreciate comfort, body positivity, and sometimes to promote sales or donations related to underwear.

Observed each year on August 5, National Underwear Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.
