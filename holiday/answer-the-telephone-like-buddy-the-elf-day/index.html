Overview

Observed annually on December 18th, "Answer The Telephone Like Buddy The Elf Day" is an unofficial, lighthearted holiday inspired by Will Ferrell's iconic character in the 2003 film "Elf." It encourages participants to channel Buddy's boundless enthusiasm by answering their phones in his distinctively cheerful and often verbose style, such as his famous greeting, "Buddy the Elf, what's your favorite color?" This day serves as a whimsical invitation to spread holiday cheer and inject humor into everyday interactions during the festive season.

Observed each year on December 18, Answer The Telephone Like Buddy The Elf Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Why it matters: Answer The Telephone Like Buddy The Elf Day" on December 18 celebrates the joy and whimsy of the holiday season by encouraging people to infuse their daily communications with the infectious enthusiasm of Buddy the Elf.

Origin and story

The 2003 film "Elf," which inspired this holiday, grossed over $220 million worldwide and has become a beloved modern Christmas classic.

Will Ferrell's memorable portrayal of Buddy the Elf, including his unique phone interactions, featured significant improvisation during filming, adding to the character's spontaneous charm.
