Overview

National Blueberry Pancake Day, observed annually on January 28th, is an unofficial holiday dedicated to celebrating the beloved breakfast dish that combines fluffy pancakes with the sweet burst of blueberries. It's a day for enthusiasts to enjoy making, sharing, and indulging in this comforting treat, often served with syrup, butter, or whipped cream, bringing a little sweetness and warmth to the winter month.

Observed each year on January 28, National Blueberry Pancake Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Blueberries are one of the few fruits native to North America, where they have been enjoyed for thousands of years.
