Overview

National Chocolate Pudding Day, observed annually on June 26th, is a delightful occasion dedicated to celebrating and enjoying the rich, creamy, and beloved dessert. Whether homemade from scratch, from a mix, or store-bought, this day encourages everyone to indulge in a bowl of chocolate pudding, perhaps topped with whipped cream, sprinkles, or fruit, honoring a timeless comfort food.

Observed each year on June 26, National Chocolate Pudding Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

The word 'pudding' originally referred to savory dishes, often meat-based, before evolving to include sweet desserts in the 17th century.

Instant chocolate pudding, which revolutionized home dessert preparation, was first introduced by Jell-O in 1947.
