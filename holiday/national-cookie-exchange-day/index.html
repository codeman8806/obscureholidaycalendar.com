Overview

National Cookie Exchange Day is a delightful occasion celebrated by baking enthusiasts who gather to share their homemade cookies. It's a day to swap recipes, enjoy a variety of treats, and spread holiday cheer through the delicious art of cookie making and gifting.

Observed each year on December 22, National Cookie Exchange Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Cookie exchanges often involve participants baking a large batch of one type of cookie to then trade for an assortment of others.

The tradition of cookie exchanges is particularly popular during the holiday season, especially in December.
