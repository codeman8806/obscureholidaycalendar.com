Overview

Visit Your Relatives Day, observed annually on May 18th, is a day dedicated to encouraging people to connect with their extended family members. It serves as a reminder to make time for those who are part of your family tree, whether through a physical visit, a phone call, or a virtual chat, fostering stronger bonds and cherishing familial relationships.

Observed each year on May 18, Visit Your Relatives Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

While its exact origins are largely informal, Visit Your Relatives Day is an unofficial observance aimed at promoting family connections.

Studies indicate that maintaining strong family ties can contribute to improved mental and emotional well-being.
