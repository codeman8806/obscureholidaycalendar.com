Overview

Lost Sock Memorial Day, observed annually on May 9th, is an unofficial, whimsical holiday dedicated to acknowledging the countless single socks that mysteriously disappear, often during the laundry cycle. It's a day for people to humorously mourn their lost sock companions and ponder the enduring mystery of where they vanish to, encouraging a moment of reflection for these unpaired garments.

Observed each year on May 9, Lost Sock Memorial Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

The holiday offers a lighthearted explanation for missing socks, often attributing their disappearance to a mythical 'laundry monster' or 'sock monster'.

Many individuals keep a 'lonely sock basket' or drawer, holding onto single socks in the persistent hope that their lost mate will eventually reappear.
