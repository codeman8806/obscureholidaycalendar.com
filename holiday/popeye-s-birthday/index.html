Popeye's Birthday ⚓

January 17 Cultural / community observance Updated 2025-12-08

This holiday is featured in the Obscure Holiday Calendar app with emoji-style visuals, reminders, and daily fun facts.

Overview

Popeye's Birthday, celebrated annually on January 17th, commemorates the first appearance of Popeye the Sailor in E.C. Segar's Thimble Theatre comic strip in 1929. This day honors the iconic spinach-loving sailor known for his strength, distinctive voice, and beloved catchphrases.

Observed each year on January 17, Popeye's Birthday invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Popeye first appeared on January 17, 1929, in the comic strip "Thimble Theatre" as a minor character, but quickly became the strip's star.

Spinach became Popeye's go-to superfood after a 1931 story established it as the source of his incredible strength.
