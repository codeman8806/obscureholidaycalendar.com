Overview

No Housework Day, observed annually on April 7th, is an unofficial holiday dedicated to encouraging individuals to take a complete break from all household chores. It serves as a lighthearted reminder to set aside cleaning, cooking, and tidying for a day, allowing for guilt-free relaxation, self-care, or leisure activities instead.

Observed each year on April 7, No Housework Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

While its exact origins are largely unknown, No Housework Day is a popular unofficial observance globally.
