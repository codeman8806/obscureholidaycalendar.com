Overview

Observed annually on July 27th, "Take Your Houseplant for a Walk Day" is an unofficial, whimsical holiday that encourages plant enthusiasts to take their potted companions outdoors. This lighthearted observance aims to provide houseplants with beneficial natural sunlight, fresh air, and varying humidity levels often absent indoors, while also offering a fun, mindful, and sometimes humorous activity for their human caregivers.

Observed each year on July 27, Take your Houseplant for a Walk Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

While its exact origins are unknown, the day is widely recognized as a playful reminder to tend to the well-being of indoor plants.

Exposing houseplants to natural outdoor light can provide a fuller spectrum of light than artificial indoor sources, benefiting photosynthesis and overall plant health.
