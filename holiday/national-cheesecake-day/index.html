Overview

National Cheesecake Day, celebrated annually on July 30th, is a special occasion dedicated to indulging in and appreciating the beloved dessert. It's a day for cheesecake enthusiasts to enjoy their favorite creamy confection, whether homemade, store-bought, or at a restaurant, often exploring various flavors and styles from classic New York to fruit-topped or chocolate-infused creations.

Observed each year on July 30, National Cheesecake Day invites people to pause, share the story, and bring a little themed joy to their day.

Origin and story

Cheesecake's origins trace back to ancient Greece, where it was served to athletes during the first Olympic Games in 776 BC.

Modern American 'New York-style' cheesecake, known for its dense, smooth, and creamy texture, typically relies heavily on cream cheese.
