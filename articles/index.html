Why Does December Have So Many Obscure Holidays?

Over the years, communities, organizations, and individuals have created unofficial celebrations to highlight causes, promote hobbies, inspire creativity, or simply add a bit of fun to everyday life. Many obscure holidays come from grassroots origins, while others were created by brands, charities, or cultural groups.

December stands out because it is traditionally a month of celebration, reflection, and gathering. As a result, countless quirky observances have taken root during this time.
