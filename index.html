Discover today’s fun, weird & obscure holidays — every single day.

Obscure Holiday Calendar is a fun daily holiday app that surfaces weird holidays, quirky national days, food holidays and more, with colorful artwork and bite-sized facts. It feels like a national day calendar alternative that fits in your pocket, complete with widgets and daily reminders.

Daily Fun, Weird & Obscure Holidays

Every morning, Obscure Holiday Calendar highlights a new obscure holiday or quirky celebration — from food days like National Taco Day to silly observances like Bubble Wrap Appreciation Day. It’s a playful mix of daily holiday reminders, fun facts and light daily trivia.

What you can explore in the app

  • Daily weird holidays, obscure national days and fun observances
  • Short descriptions and fun facts you can read in a few seconds
  • Colorful, scroll-stopping holiday artwork
  • Optional notifications so you never miss today’s holiday
  • Home Screen widgets (on supported devices) to see the holiday at a glance
  • Easy sharing so you can send holidays to friends, family or your class

Who the Obscure Holiday Calendar app is for

The app is perfect for:

  • Parents looking for a quick, fun moment with kids
  • Teachers who want a light icebreaker or writing prompt in class
  • Trivia fans and people who love random daily facts
  • Social media creators who post about weird holidays
  • Anyone who enjoys holiday widget apps and fun iOS / Android widgets

Why people enjoy this daily holiday app

Obscure Holiday Calendar keeps things easy: one main holiday per day, clean artwork, and a short description. It’s a simple habit that’s easy to stick with, and it makes a great companion to other daily trivia apps or national day calendar style sites.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes, Obscure Holiday Calendar is free to download on both iOS and Android. Optional in-app purchases or rewarded ads may unlock extra features like an ad-free experience.

Does it work on both iPhone and Android?

Yes. The app is available on the Apple App Store for iPhone and iPad and on Google Play for Android phones and tablets.

Can I use it as a daily reminder?

You can enable notifications and widgets so today’s obscure holiday appears automatically, making it a fun daily holiday reminder.

Is the content family friendly?

Yes. The app is designed to stay light, fun and family-friendly, making it suitable for parents, kids, teachers and classrooms.
