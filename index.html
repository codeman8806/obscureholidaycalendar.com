Discover Fun, Weird & Obscure Holidays Every Day

Obscure Holiday Calendar is the easiest and most delightful way to discover quirky, fun, and unusual holidays celebrated around the world. Whether you're interested in food holidays, national days, weird traditions, fun facts, or daily trivia, this app gives you a fresh celebration every morning.

Features

  • Daily obscure holidays with beautiful artwork

  • Holiday descriptions for kids, adults, families, and teachers
  • Shareable holiday cards for friends & social media
  • Clean holiday calendar layout

  • Perfect for daily trivia lovers and social content creators

Why People Love This App

Each day reveals a new celebration like National Taco Day, Puzzle Day, Jukebox Day, World Kindness Day, Cookie Day, and hundreds more. It’s perfect for classrooms, workplaces, social posts, or simply making mornings more fun.
