What the bot does

/today & /date

Show today’s holiday or any date (MM-DD) with emoji, description, and a link to your site.

/search & /random

Find holidays by keyword (e.g., “bacon day”) or let the bot surprise you.

/facts

Quick fun facts pulled from your holiday library to keep chats lively.

Daily channel posts

Auto-posts today’s holidays to a channel you choose, with a teaser for tomorrow.

How to add it

  • Step 1: Click “Invite to Discord.”
  • Step 2: Choose your server and approve (needs Send Messages + Embed Links).
  • Step 3: Try /today, /date, or /random right away. Daily posts run once configured by the bot owner.
