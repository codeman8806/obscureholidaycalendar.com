Discord holiday bot FAQ

Is it free?

Yes. You can add the Discord holiday bot for free. Premium adds multiple channels, advanced scheduling, and premium commands.

What permissions are required?

Send Messages and Embed Links are required so holiday cards and links render correctly.

Does it support daily holiday posts?

Yes. The bot can auto-post daily holidays to a channel you choose (free tier posts once daily at 00:00 UTC).

Can I choose the posting channel?

Yes. The bot posts to the channel you select, with premium control over time, timezone, and multiple channels.

Premium Discord bot features and upgrades

Premium unlocks /date, /search, /random, /facts, /tomorrow, /upcoming, /week, multiple daily channels, custom timezone/hour, branding toggle, and advanced scheduling.
