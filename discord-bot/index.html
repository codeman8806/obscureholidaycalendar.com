Discord holiday bot FAQ

Is it free?

Yes. You can add the bot for free with /today, /fact, /streak, /share, and daily posts. Premium adds advanced scheduling, analytics, and extra commands.

What permissions are required?

Send Messages and Embed Links are required. Add Reactions is recommended for streaks and engagement analytics.

Does it support daily holiday posts?

Yes. The bot auto-posts daily holidays to a channel you choose (free tier posts once daily at 00:00 UTC).

What is premium?

Premium adds advanced commands, analytics, tone selection, filters, server lore, surprise days, and multi-channel scheduling.

Premium Discord bot features and upgrades

Premium unlocks /tomorrow, /upcoming, /week, /date, /search, /random, /facts, /analytics, /lore, tone selection, filters, surprise days, multiple channels, custom timezone/hour, branding controls, and advanced scheduling.
