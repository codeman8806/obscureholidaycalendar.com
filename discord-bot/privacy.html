Privacy Policy for the Obscure Holiday Discord Bot

Effective date: December 2025

What we collect

  • Discord IDs: server, channel, and user IDs needed to deliver slash commands and daily posts.
  • Preferences you set: timezone, hour, channel, role mentions, embed style/color, holiday choice, and premium status.
  • Operational logs: minimal error logs and request timestamps for troubleshooting and rate limits. We do not log message content.
  • Billing (premium only): payments are handled by Stripe. We receive subscription status and guild ID metadata; card details stay with Stripe.

How we use your data

  • To respond to slash commands (e.g., /today, /date, /setup).
  • To schedule and send daily holiday posts in the channel you choose.
  • To enforce rate limits and prevent abuse.
  • To manage premium access tied to your server.

How long we keep it

  • Guild/channel configuration is kept until you remove the bot or ask us to delete it.
  • Error logs are short-lived and rotated regularly.
  • Subscription records follow Stripe’s required retention for billing and tax.

What we do not collect

  • No message content from your channels.
  • No member conversations or attachments.
  • No location or advertising identifiers.

Data sharing

  • Discord provides user/guild/channel IDs and command payloads.
  • Stripe processes payments and stores billing data when you subscribe.
  • We do not sell or trade data with third parties.

Security

Access to bot configuration and billing data is limited to the bot operator. API tokens and keys are kept in environment variables; access is restricted to trusted services.

Your choices

  • Remove the bot: removing the bot from your server stops data collection and daily posts.
  • Delete data: email sea.graves@mail.com with your server ID and request deletion of stored configuration.
  • Opt out of premium: cancel via the billing portal link in the bot or on the bot page. Stripe handles billing records.

Changes

We may update this policy for clarity or to reflect new features. Continued use of the bot after updates means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Email sea.graves@mail.com or visit the contact page with questions about this policy.
