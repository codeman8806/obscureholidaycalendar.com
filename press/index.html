What is Obscure Holiday Calendar?

Obscure Holiday Calendar is a structured, searchable library of fun, weird, and specific observances—paired with category hubs, daily widgets, and lightweight facts. It helps audiences discover timely ideas and gives journalists a consistent reference for “national day” coverage.

Our dataset powers holiday pages, category rollups, and the annual National Day Report used for editorial planning and trend analysis.
